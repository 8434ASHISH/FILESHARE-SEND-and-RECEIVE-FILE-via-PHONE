Privacy Policy

FileShare P2P

← Back to FileShare

1. Overview

FileShare P2P is a peer-to-peer file sharing platform designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not permanently store files on our servers. All transfers use direct WebRTC connections between peers.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data:

  • Room codes (temporary, deleted after session ends)
  • Session information (connection duration)
  • Basic analytics (file types, transfer success rates)

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only for:

  • Facilitating peer-to-peer connections
  • Service improvement and reliability
  • Preventing abuse and maintaining platform security

4. Data Security

WebRTC connections between peers are encrypted end-to-end. Room codes are temporary and invalidated after transfers complete. We use industry-standard security practices to protect our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare Workers for signaling. Please review their privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data. Contact us at contact@fileshare-p2p.local with your request.

7. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, visit our contact page.
