Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Fileshare is a peer-to-peer file transfer service. We do not store your files on any server. Files are transferred directly between devices using WebRTC technology.

2. How We Use Information

We use PeerJS for establishing connections. Temporary peer IDs are generated for session identification only and expire after disconnection.

3. Data Security

All file transfers are end-to-end encrypted using DTLS-SRTP protocol. We cannot access your files during transfer.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for connection management. Advertising partners may use their own tracking technologies.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Monetag for advertisements. Please refer to their privacy policy for ad-related data collection.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us through the Contact Us form.
